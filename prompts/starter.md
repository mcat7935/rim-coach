# RIM Starter Coaching Prompt (v0 — scaffolding)

> ⚠️ Scaffolding only. Content under development.

For users who are stuck at the start of drawing a RIM diagram.

## Intended use

For someone who has a topic but doesn't know how to begin structuring it as a RIM diagram. The prompt helps them surface:

- What viewpoint(s) they're starting from (UX / business / development)
- What elements feel central vs. peripheral
- Where they sense a potential cycle
- What they would *expect* to see if the structure "worked"

## Discipline

Same as `review.md`: **hints, not answers**. Especially important at the starter stage — leading questions kill the user's own thinking.
